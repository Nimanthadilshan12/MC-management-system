About Us

Welcome to the University of Ruhuna Medical Centre, your trusted partner in health and wellness.

Our Mission

At the University of Ruhuna Medical Centre, our mission is to provide high-quality, accessible healthcare services to students, faculty, and the community. We are committed to promoting wellness, preventing illness, and delivering compassionate care with professionalism and integrity.

Our Services

  • General and specialized medical consultations
  • Comprehensive pharmacy services
  • Health screenings and preventive care
  • Emergency medical support
  • Health education and counseling

Our Team

Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals includes experienced doctors, pharmacists, and administrative staff who work together to ensure the best care for our patients. We collaborate with the University of Ruhuna to integrate academic excellence with healthcare delivery.
